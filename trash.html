Makhana (Fox Nuts)

Makhana, also known as Fox Nuts or Lotus Seeds, is a nutritious and healthy food harvested from lotus plants grown in freshwater ponds. It is widely consumed for its light texture, natural taste, and health benefits.

Makhana is low in fat, gluten-free, and rich in protein, fiber, calcium, magnesium, and antioxidants. It is commonly used for roasted snacks, fasting foods, sweets, and traditional Indian dishes like kheer and curries.

The product is graded based on Sutta (size), which indicates the number of pieces per 100 grams:

  • 3 Sutta: Extra-large, premium quality, best for roasting
  • 4 Sutta: Large size, ideal for retail and branded snacks
  • 5 Sutta: Medium size, suitable for daily use and cooking
  • 6 Sutta: Small size, economical, best for bulk and sweets
