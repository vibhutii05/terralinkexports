Makhana (Fox Nuts) - Nature's Superfood

Makhana is naturally low in fat, gluten-free, and packed with protein, fiber, calcium, magnesium, and antioxidants. It's perfect for health-conscious consumers, athletes, and those following traditional or modern dietary practices.
